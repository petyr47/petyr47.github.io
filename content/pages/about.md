## Hi there 👋

My name is Peter, and I'm a senior mobile developer. I build Android native apps with
Kotlin, iOS native apps with Swift and cross-platform apps with KMP and Flutter, and I care about clean, observable,
and maintainable code.

I use this site to write up what I learn and to keep track of projects I'm
working on. Some of my longer-form writing also lives over on
[Hackernoon](https://hackernoon.com/u/petyr) and [Substack](https://peteraneke.substack.com).

## What I work with

- **Languages:** Kotlin, Java, Swift, Dart.
- **Android:** Jetpack Compose, AOSP, Coroutines, Gradle, Hilt, Koin, Retrofit, Room, etc.
- **iOS:**: Swift, SwiftUI, Objective-C.
- **Cross-platform:** KMP, Flutter, Firebase, Ktor etc.
- **Backend:** Spring-Boot, Ktor, PostgreSQL.
- **Tooling:** GitHub Actions, Sentry, SonarQube, Figma, Fastlane.
- **APIs:** REST, GraphQl, OpenAPI/Swagger, Postman.
